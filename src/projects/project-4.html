About Myself

Personal profile site that works like a portfolio, but focuses more on who I am, what shaped me, and what defines my perspective.

Project Snapshot

Course/Context: WEB 2890

Role: Accessibility reviewer and front-end implementer

Tools: HTML, SCSS, JavaScript

Live Demo: web2890-ajs-review-assignment.netlify.app

GitHub: github.com/WSU-SoC-WEB2890-CU/web2890-review-assignment-AJSpicknall.git

Challenge

Many personal websites feel generic and fail to communicate personality, values, and story in a meaningful way.

Solution

I designed a content flow, centered on personal background, goals, strengths, and interests, with a visual style that feels authentic and direct.

Result

The finished site presents a stronger personal identity and gives visitors a clearer sense of who I am beyond just technical projects.
