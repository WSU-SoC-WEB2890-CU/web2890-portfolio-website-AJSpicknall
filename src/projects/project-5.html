Buy and Car Site

Car dealership-style site where users can list a vehicle, submit an offer, and move through a simple sell-your-car process.

Project Snapshot

Course/Context: WEB 2890

Role: Interaction designer and developer

Tools: HTML, SCSS, JavaScript

Live Demo: web2890-ajs-project-1.netlify.app

GitHub: github.com/WSU-SoC-WEB2890-CU/web2890-project-1-AJSpicknall.git

Challenge

Car selling flows can feel confusing when valuation steps, offer details, and contact actions are spread across disconnected pages.

Solution

Built a focused flow with clear form sections for vehicle details, offer inputs, and next-step actions so users can quickly submit and review terms.

Result

The final experience made the sell-and-offer process easier to follow and improved clarity around how a car submission turns into a purchase offer.
