Disability Site

Research-focused disability site explaining key studies, lived experiences, and why accessibility knowledge matters in modern development.

Project Snapshot

Course/Context: WEB 2700

Role: Visual designer and developer

Tools: HTML, SCSS, JavaScript

Live Demo: ajs-final-project-web2700.netlify.app

GitHub: github.com/WEB-3130/web-2700-final-website-AJSpicknall.git

Challenge

Disability research and accessibility findings are often presented in ways that are difficult for developers to discover and apply in real projects.

Solution

Built a clear an educational structure that highlights major studies, practical takeaways, and direct links between research outcomes and inclusive coding decisions.

Result

The final site made complex research easier to understand and gave other future developers a sample on how to build a more inclusive digital site.
