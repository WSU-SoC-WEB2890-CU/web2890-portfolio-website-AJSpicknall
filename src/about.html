About Me

I am AJ Spicknall, a WebUX student focused on front-end development, interaction design, and practical accessibility. My goal is to build digital experiences that are clear, useful, and visually intentional.

Background

I combine design and development skills to move projects from concept to coded delivery. My strongest work comes from projects where the visual system, content strategy, and interaction details are planned together from the beginning.

Core Skills

  • Semantic HTML and maintainable CSS architecture
  • Responsive layout with mobile-first planning
  • JavaScript for interface behavior and interaction feedback
  • Wireframing, prototype iteration, and usability testing
  • Accessibility checks with keyboard and contrast review

Professional Goals

I am preparing for front-end and UX-focused roles where design quality and technical execution are both expected. I care about writing clean code, documenting decisions, and shipping projects that are easy to maintain after launch.
